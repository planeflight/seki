Hikes

Sequoia National Park has some of the most amazing hikes in California!

Lakes Trail

The Lakes Trail is one of the most popular wilderness hikes in Sequoia National Park. You will climb from the Wolverton trailhead to three gorgeous alpine lakes: Heather, Emerald, and Pear. Aster lake is not far off the trail and can be viewed from the trail as well! This route can be done as a day trip on horseback or on foot. Hikers can make this into an overnight backpacking trip, but horseback riding and pack use is limited to day use only. This route follows the Lake Trail for most of the way, with a short stint on a segment known as the "Hump Trail".
